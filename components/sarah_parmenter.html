Sarah Parmenter

A Little Bit About Her

I was first exposed to Sarah Parmenter through her podcast Happy Monday. On that show she discusses a large array of topics in the Front-End design world. She is mostly known for her work with UI design for mobile and iOS. She's from England and runs the design firm You Know Who. In 2011, she won "Designer of the Year" at the .net Magazine Awards. She speaks at many conferences every year including An Event Apart conferences. Her personal website can be found at Sazzy.co.uk.
